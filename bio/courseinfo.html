Textbook and optional references

Prerequisites

Good knowledge of convex optimization (as in 22494), and exposure to bioinformatics.
You will use one of the COBRA toolbox (Matlab), COBRApy (Python), or COBRA.jl (Julia), to write simple scripts, so basic familiarity with elementary programming will be required. We refer to COBRA, COBRApy, and Convex.jl collectively as COBRA*.
